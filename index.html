Have you ever wondered a day on which you were born on? All is easy to know with Akan Names.

Akan Names are the names that are commonly derived from Ghanian culture, and they are given to the babies depending on the day that they are born on. For instance, if a baby boy is born on Tues, he is given a name of Kwabena. If it's a girl, she is given a name of Abena.
